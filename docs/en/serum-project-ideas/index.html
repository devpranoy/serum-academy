Project Ideas for Serum

The nodes recently voted in favor of the this Proposal, creating an initiative to fund projects building on Serum coordinated by EcoSerum, one of Project Serum’s nodes. Furthermore, 2% of all Serum fees will be used to fund new projects through EcoSerum.
