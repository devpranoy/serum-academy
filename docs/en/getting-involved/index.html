EcoSerum

All projects built on Serum are eligible for an EcoSerum grant

About

EcoSerum brings active investors, traders and developers together to build the Serum ecosystem. As a new, decentralized exchange, Serum is only as strong as its liquidity, accessibility and community. Our stakers are active SRM holders committed to seeing Project Serum flourish, supporting the development of UX focused tools and applications.
